About
Transit Oriented Development (TOD)

Transit Oriented Development (TOD) has been a trend that promotes more housing and mixed-use amenities near public transportation. While such development can potentially increase efficiency of transit system operation, it can also be beneficial for land value capitalization which would generate more property tax revenue for the city. This application will visually illustrate the impacts for different properties through maps and charts.

About TOD in SF

TOD in SF is created by two graduate city planning students for a web mapping course at the University of Pennsylvania's Weitzman School of Design.
